The Lonesome Crowded West1

At 31, I'm now a seasoned nomad. I've visited four-fifths of this beautiful country, I've lived in six different cities in five different states since I turned 182, and I've made a home in every city my heart told me I should — except one: San Francisco, California. Until Now.

I am so pleased to announce that today I join an amazing company full of brilliant people with brilliant ideas: Nest Labs. Technology has such power to meaningfully improve the world around us and Nest is doing exactly. Whether it's saving energy or saving lives, Nest is engineering a better world daily. I am humbled and elated to join their team.

I am also excited to join my former campaigners—Matt Gipp, Sean Kealey, and Manik Rathee—under such wonderful circumstances. Not only are they three of the best humans I know, but they are also all tremendously talented. I can't wait to build some really incredible things along side them once again.

Mountain Monday is back.
