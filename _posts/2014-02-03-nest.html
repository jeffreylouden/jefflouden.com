The Lonesome Crowded West1

At 31, I'm now a seasoned nomad. I've lived in six different cities in five different states since I turned 182 and have been lucky enough to live in every city I once dreamed of calling home, except one: San Francisco, California. Until Now.

I am so pleased to announce that this week I'm beginning a new journey with an amazing company full of brilliant people with brilliant ideas: Nest Labs. Technology has such power to meaningfully improve the world around us and Nest is doing exactly that. Whether it's saving energy or potentially saving lives, Nest is engineering a better world. I am humbled and elated to join the team.

I am also excited to join my former campaigners—Matt Gipp, Sean Kealey, and Manik Rathee—under such wonderful circumstances. Not only are they three of the best humans I know, but they are also all tremendously talented. I can't wait to build some really incredible things alongside them once again. Mountain Monday is back.

Dear New York City,

It's not you, it's me. Can we still be friends? You were nothing but wonderful. You exceeded my every expectation. I am very heartbroken to leave you, but I have to do what's best for me.

To all the people I leave behind (who are too numerous to name individually3), I promise to keep in touch. I am better for having known every one of you and I will see you again very soon.
