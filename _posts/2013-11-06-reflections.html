November 6, 2012

A really wonderful girl told me recently "It felt like it was much easier for you to leave me than it was for you to leave the campaign." It feels impossible to disconnect the 2012 Presidential election as division of my first 30 years. There was everything before and now everything after. Everyone has these moments, but this is the first one that I'll simply never be able to shake and that time I'll talk about indefinitely.

Photo by Manik Rathee

In second successive year drowning with change and sleepless nights, it's true yet again that the people you surround yourself with tend to make all the difference and a few of them deserve my gratitude: Mary, Kyle, Grant, Dan, Sasha, O'neil, David, Pam, Nick, Wyatt, Kelly, Alexa, Jason, Matt, Bruce, and all of the amazing people I know I've forgot to mention.

"The really important kind of freedom involves attention, and awareness, and discipline, and effort, and being able truly to care about other people and to sacrifice for them, over and over, in myriad petty little unsexy ways, every day."

David Foster Wallace, This is Water
